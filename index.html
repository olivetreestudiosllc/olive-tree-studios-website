Who are we?

Blender may be free, but making ideas happen is not!

We are a fledgling independent studio with one simple goal — empowering those with ideas by providing the resources and structure to make them happen. Starting with Olive Avenue as our flagship series, we’re laying a foundation to support that goal. Olive Tree Studios is made possible by independent commissioned collaborators across the globe, working together to make Olive Avenue and beyond a reality.

Where to see?

Watch our productions free of charge on YouTube! Or, alternatively, we welcome the support on our Patreon page, which not only provides awesome exclusive benefits, but also allows for extended or unmodified versions of our productions too.

Our Productions

Set in a suburban Pennsylvania town, Olive Avenue follows teens/young adults Roy Strofferson and Nate Riley on their journey through everything the late 2010s and early 2020s have to offer. A modern animated take on the humor found in King of the Hill and Seinfeld.
