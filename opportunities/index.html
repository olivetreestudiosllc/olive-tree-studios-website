What is an “independent commissioned contributor”?

Even in its fledgling state, Olive Tree Studios is already made up of a global canvas collective. Artists, worldwide, contributing to something greater than themselves. We aren't about to limit ourselves to talent just within the United States.

An independent commissioned contributor is how we get our productions made. We commission independent artists that have been vetted and onboarded as contributors to our productions. These independent artists are supplied with resources, knowledge, and feedback to bring our productions to life. And we're constantly looking for more as we go.

We provide our contributors with time/project management and cloud based resources, to ensure ducks are in a row and contributing artists are valued well.

We're looking for passionate artists who are looking to help build our productions and carry out visions, while offering professional credible experience. If this sounds like something you want to be a part of, check out our open positions below!
